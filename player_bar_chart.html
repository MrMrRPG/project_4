Summary: Top 10 players by ratings in 2000


A chess rating system is significant because it is used to estimate the strength of a player based on their performance against other players. In 2000, the historical moment happened when Krammik defeated Kasparov at the Brain Games World Chess Championship despite the fact that Kasparov still reigned as the player with the highest performance rating up until 2002.
